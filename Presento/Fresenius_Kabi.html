Fresenius Kabi

Fresenius Kabi, established in 1995 as a wholly owned subsidiary of Fresenius SE & Co. KGaA, is a global healthcare company specializing in lifesaving medicines and technologies for infusion, transfusion, and clinical nutrition. Their diverse range of products and services includes biopharmaceuticals, biosimilars, generic drugs, enteral and parenteral nutrition, medical devices, and more.

Infusion Pumps

Fresenius Kabi Products

Volumetric Pumps

These pumps are used to deliver larger volumes of fluid to a patient at a set flow rate. They can also operate in different infusion modes, such as volume + flow rate, volume + time, flow rate + time, and volume + time + rate. They can also display the drug name and parameters based on a drug library of up to 120 drugs. They have a pause function that can be programmed from 1 minute to 24 hours and a night mode that decreases the brightness of the screen and the key beep

Syringe Pumps

These pumps are used to gradually administer small amounts of fluid to a patient or for use in chemical and biomedical research. They can operate in different infusion modes, such as ml/h, dose rate, volume or dose/time, and bolus. They can also display the drug name and parameters based on a list of 50 pre-set drug names or a drug library of up to 120 drugs. They have a Dynamic Pressure System (DPS) that warns of pressure variations and an anti-bolus system that reduces bolus after occlusion release.
