COMPANY INTRODUCTION

In 2018 REACH MEDICAL SYSTEMS PRIVATE LIMITED company was incorporated after the merger of Orient Medi Systems in Vijayawada, Andhrapradesh and Eternal Medical Solutions in Secundrabad, Telangana. Our core team was at work for over 20 years. Withholding the legacy and reputation built, Reach Medical Systems Private Limited continued its operations across Andhra Pradesh and Telangana with intense passion and great enthusiasm. From the beginning, our team had been carefully knitting all the human relations globally to establish our vision and mission by associating with various global brands that manufacture vital healthcare systems that assist clinicians. While engaging with healthcare professionals and experts at global MNC companies, our team is committed to learning from the beginning about the demands of healthcare systems as each day progresses. Brands in our expanding consortium were chosen carefully by our experts in the team to ensure the quality, durability and serviceability of the products which REACH imports that will provide solutions to the demands. In anticipation of serving the healthcare community, our best.
