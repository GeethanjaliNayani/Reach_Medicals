IITPL

IITPL stands for Innovation Imaging Technologies Pvt. Ltd., a company that manufactures catheterisation laboratory systems, also known as cath labs. Cath labs are used for performing various procedures on the heart and blood vessels, such as angiography, angioplasty, stenting, and pacemaker implantation. It was founded in 2016 by a group of experienced professionals from the medical device industry, who have held leadership roles in Philips, Abbott, J&J, and VJ Technologies. It is one of India’s fastest growing medical device companies, with over 200 units sold and 155 units installed in 49 months. It has also won 11 national and international awards for its innovative cath lab products. It has a strong R&D capability, with over 500 engineers and scientists working on developing innovative products and solutions. It also has a social responsibility mission, supporting various initiatives to improve access to quality healthcare and education for underserved communities.

IITPL Products

Pinnacle Agile

A side-mounted cath lab system that offers variable detector movement, table swivel, full body coverage, and high definition detector panel. It is the first in its segment to offer these features and is suitable for a wide variety of vascular procedures.

Premier Elite and Select

A fixed flat panel cath lab system that offers optimized radiation, tablet-based control, and advanced features such as digital stent enhancement and QCA. It is the world’s smallest fixed flat panel cath lab and delivers sharp and noise-free images at low radiation dose.
