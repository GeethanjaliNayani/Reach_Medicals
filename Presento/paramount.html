Paramount Bed

Paramount Bed was established in 1947 in Japan and has since expanded its business to various countries such as China, India, Brazil, Mexico, Vietnam, and Singapore. Paramount Bed offers comprehensive solutions for clients from critical care to home care, with products such as electric beds, bedside cabinets, mattresses, fall prevention alarms, and delivery beds. Paramount Bed has a subsidiary in India called Paramount Bed India Private Limited, which was incorporated in 2012 and is located in Gurgaon, Haryana.
