Aerogen

Aerogen was founded in 1997 in Ireland and has since expanded its operations to various countries such as the US, China, Germany, and India. Aerogen’s patented Vibronic aerosol technology is an integral part of its drug delivery systems, which allow drugs to be nebulized into a fine particle mist that can be absorbed through the lungs while maintaining drug integrity. Aerogen’s products are used in over 75 countries worldwide and have helped over 16 million respiratory patients in acute care settings such as emergency departments, intensive care units, and neonatal units and commercializing novel therapies for respiratory distress syndrome in premature infants.

NEBULISERS

Aerogen Solo

This single-patient-use device enables aerosol drug delivery across various respiratory stages, including IMV, NIV, HF, and SV. Its unique palladium vibrating mesh nebulizes drugs into fine particles, optimizing lung absorption while maintaining drug integrity.

View Brochure

Aerogen Pro

This is a reusable device that delivers high performance aerosol drug delivery for multiple patients. It has a robust design that can withstand repeated use and sterilization. It can be used with various ventilator circuits and interfaces.

View Brochure

Aerogen Ultra

This is a handheld device used in conjunction with the Aerogen Solo to deliver inhalation treatment either post-ventilation or during exacerbations. It has a soft-seal mouthpiece that ensures optimal aerosol delivery and reduces drug wastage.

View Brochure

Aerogen's T-Piece

Aerogen's T-Piece is a device that connects the Aerogen Solo nebuliser or Pro nebuliser to various interfaces such as ventilator circuits, face masks, and mouthpieces. It allows aerosol drug delivery to patients who are either ventilated or self-ventilating.

Aerogen Pro X controller

This reusable aerosol drug delivery device, designed for durability and sterilization, performs effectively for multiple patients. It is compatible with various ventilator circuits, operates in two modes (30-minute continuous and 6-hour intermittent aerosol delivery), and is powered by an AC/DC adapter or battery pack.

Aerogen USB controller

This portable aerosol drug delivery device supports all respiratory stages (IMV, NIV, HF, SV) with a slim design, USB compatibility for ventilators, and two modes (30 minutes, 6 hours). Powered exclusively by an AC/DC adapter.
