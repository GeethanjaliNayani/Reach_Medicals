Ventilator-VG70

Ventilators: These devices are used to deliver oxygen and other gases to the lungs of patients who cannot breathe normally or need assistance. Aeonmed produces ICU ventilators, emergency and transport ventilators, and homecare ventilators. They have advanced technologies such as adaptive ventilation, lung protective ventilation, non-invasive ventilation, and high flow oxygen therapy. They also have user-friendly interfaces, data management systems, and alarm functions.

  • It has a special alloy breathing system that is easy to remove and clean, fully autoclavable, and has a constant temperature heating system to prevent moisture condensation.
  • It has a low tidal volume of 10 ml in volume control ventilation, which provides more options for low tidal volume ventilation to benefit the patient
  • It has an individualized PEEP and minimized impact recruitment maneuver to protect the lungs.
  • It has a compliance and leakage compensation, fresh gas compensation, and altitude compensation to ensure accurate ventilation delivery.
  • It has a trigger function that allows the patient to initiate the breath by pressure or flow.
  • It has an inspiratory plateau function that allows the clinician to adjust the inspiratory time percentage.
  • It has a Tslope function that allows the clinician to adjust the slope of the pressure waveform.
  • It has an AORV mode that provides automatic oxygenation and recruitment ventilation for patients with severe ARDS.
