WATO EX-65 Workstation

The Mindray WATO EX-65 Anesthesia Machine is a feature-rich system designed to meet the needs of a wide range of patients.

Features:

  • Smarter Ventilator Technology: The WATO EX-65 is equipped with smarter ventilator technology that supports precise anesthesia and comprehensive ventilation modes.
  • Enhanced Monitoring Capabilities: It has enhanced monitoring capabilities to fully meet the needs of all your patients.
  • Fresh Gas Flow Optimizer: This indicates the recommended fresh gas flow to ensure safe low flow and minimize waste of anesthetic agents and medical gases.
  • Lung Recruitment Function: This includes two optional maneuvers and multiple pieces of information to evaluate recruitment effectiveness.
  • Integrated, Heated Breathing System: This reduces rain-out to a minimum, providing improved flow sensor accuracy and guaranteeing extensive stability of tidal volume delivery.
  • 15-inch Capacitive Touchscreen: This high-resolution touchscreen allows users to view and configure parameters as required.
  • Large Easy-to-clean Worktable: This provides an optimized work surface for writing all your documents and keeping drugs and intubation equipment conveniently to hand.
  • Plug-and-play Multi-Gas Module and BIS Monitoring: These are additional functionalities of the compact WATO EX-65.
  • Integrated Suction System: This is an optional feature built-in for your convenience.

    • Advantages:

  • User-Friendly Interface: The easy-to-use UI supports informed decision making through clearly displayed graphical and numerical data.
  • Cost-Effective: The WATO EX-65 Pro is designed with cost-effectiveness in mind, making it easier to monitor anesthetic agent consumption and even predict the trends of EtAA.
  • Precise Control: With new integrated innovative functions, the WATO EX-65 Pro enables you to precisely control anesthesia for different types of patients easily and precisely.

    • Benefits:

  • Suitable for a Wide Range of Patients: The WATO EX-65 is suitable for general anesthesia in a wide range of operating theatre patients from infants to adults.
  • Reduces Incidence of Postoperative Pulmonary Complications: The new Lung Recruitment function helps to reduce the incidence of postoperative pulmonary complications.
  • Space-Saving Design: The compact WATO EX-65 has a space-saving design with even more functionalities.
  • Improved Flow Sensor Accuracy: The integrated, heated breathing system provides improved flow sensor accuracy.
  • Streamlined Workflow: The flat-menu structure reduces the amount of interaction required to make control and parameter changes, streamlining your workflow.
  • Convenience: The optional patient suction system is built in for your convenience.
  • Smaller Footprint: The WATO EX-65 anesthesia machine has a smaller footprint.
