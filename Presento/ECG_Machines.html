Mindray ECG Machines

Mindray, a Chinese multinational medical instrumentation manufacturer established in 1991, operates in three key business lines: Patient Monitoring & Life Support, In-Vitro Diagnostic Products, and Medical Imaging Systems. As China's largest medical device manufacturer, Mindray has a global presence in over 190 countries. They offer products such as Ultrasound, X-ray, MRI, CT systems, and nuclear medicine systems for medical imaging. Mindray's vision is to enhance global healthcare by developing and sharing medical technologies and solutions.
